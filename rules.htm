Globe Telecom / Innove Communications Auction Terms and Conditions

1.  Bid price details

    The bid price submitted by the supplier shall be exclusive of VAT.

2.  Price validity

    The price quoted must be valid and firm for a period of 90 days. No change in price quoted shall be allowed
    after bid submission unless negotiated by Globe Telecom/Innove Communications.

3.  Price confirmation

    All auction participants must submit the price breakdown according to the final bid price submitted during
    the auction event not later than 24 hours after the auction event has ended. The sum of the breakdown
    must be equal to the supplier’s final bid price submitted during the auction event and must be complete
    and compliant to the Bill of Quantity (BOQ) provided by Globe/Innove. Any attempt to submit a breakdown
    which totals significantly higher or lower than the final bid price submitted during the bid event, or any
    breakdown with missing line item/s may be subject to sanctions from Globe Telecom/Innove Communications
    which includes disqualification of the submitted bid.

4.  Grounds for invalidating bids

    A supplier's bid may be invalidated under any of the following circumstances:

  • Incomplete bid documents.
  • Bid documents without bidder's signature.
  • Late submission of hard copy of bid price breakdown.

5.  Awarding of Bid

    The lowest bidder is not necessarily the winning bidder. Globe Telecom/Innove Communications shall not be bound
    to assign any reason for not accepting any bid or accepting it in part. Bids are still subject to further evaluation.
    Globe Telecom/Innove Communications shall award the winning supplier through a Purchase Order.

6.  Penalties (depends on the items to be purchased)

  • 10K – 49.99K Php         500.00 for every day of delay
  • 50K – 99.99K Php         1,000.00 for every day of delay
  • 100K – 199.99K Php     2,000.00 for every day of delay
  • 200K – 299.99K Php     3,000.00 for every day of delay
  • 300K – 499.99K Php     4,000.00 for every day of delay
  • 500K – 999.99K Php     5,000.00 for every day of delay
  • 1M – 1.999M Php          15,000.00 for every day of delay
  • 2M – 19.999M Php        20,000.00 for every day of delay
  • 20M above                    1/10 of 1% of the contract price

Globe Telecom / Innove Communications Auction Rules & Regulations
  • The supplier must confirm receipt and compliance to this auction invitation by replying through
    the e-Sourcing Portal. In case he is unable to participate in the said event, the reason thereof
    should be stated. The invited supplier must notify the Commodity Specialist of its response to
    the invitation prior to the auction date.
  • The supplier should inform the Commodity Specialist, through the e-Sourcing Portal, of any
    question regarding the auction event prior to its start date. Questions will only be entertained
    by the Commodity Specialist prior to the auction date. Any clarification raised while the auction
    event is in progress will not be entertained.
  • All bid tenders submitted shall be valid and binding on the supplier. Once submitted, the bids
    can no longer be withdrawn. Caution and reasonable care are therefore advised when
    submitting bids.
  • Bid tenders are confidential. Suppliers are not allowed to discuss their bids with the assigned
    Commodity Specialist, any Globe Telecom employee, other suppliers, or any other outside entity
    during the auction event.
  • In case of an occurrence of a technical fault on the e-Sourcing system, which prevents the entry
    of any tender from the supplier, the event shall be halted, and rescheduled at a specified date and
    time by the Commodity Specialist. The re-started Auction event shall commence with the lowest
    submitted tender at the time of suspension as the starting price.
  • The supplier cannot request the Commodity Specialist to suspend an ongoing Auction event for
    any reason. The responsibility of ensuring the hardware, software (e.g. Internet browser) and
    communication infrastructure requirements to connect to the e-Sourcing Portal rests on the supplier.
  • This auction invitation by Globe Telecom/Innove Communications does not denote compliance to
    Globe Telecom/Innove Communications technical, commercial or other requirements in any manner.
  • Globe Telecom/Innove Communications reserves the right to cancel an upcoming Auction event at
    its sole discretion, or reject any or all bids submitted on an event.
  • The submission of the lowest bid does not automatically signify the award of a purchase. All auction
    participants must submit the price breakdown according to the final bid price submitted during the
    auction event not later than 24 hours after the auction event has ended. The final written bid prices
    will be evaluated by Globe Telecom/Innove Communications and the offer most favorable to Globe
    Telecom will be awarded the purchase.
